Return Policy for MangaShop

Effective Date: 19. 10. 2024

At MangaShop, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not entirely happy with your order, you may return it under the following conditions:

1. Eligibility for Returns

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of receipt.
  • Items must be in their original condition: unopened, unused, and with all original packaging and tags attached.

2. Non-Returnable Items

  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable software products
  • Items marked as "final sale" or "non-returnable"

3. Return Process

To initiate a return:

  1. Contact our customer service at mangashop@bankai.com to request a return authorization.
  2. Include your order number and the reason for the return.
  3. Once your request is approved, you will receive further instructions on how to return your item.

4. Return Shipping

  • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to a mistake on our part (e.g., defective or incorrect items).
  • We recommend using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance for returns over $75, as we cannot guarantee that we will receive your returned item.

5. Refunds

  • Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.
  • Approved refunds will be processed and credited back to your original method of payment within 5-10 business days.

6. Exchanges

If you need to exchange an item for the same item in a different size or color, please follow the return process above and place a new order for the desired item.

Thank you for shopping at MangaShop! If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to us.
